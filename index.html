ღ an aside about the meaning of bread to me ღ

Once when I was 13 years young my mother confessed that she almost loved me more than she loved bread.
With her eyes closed, she swayed back in forth in rapture, professing her love for bread in the key of G.
I have fantasies of sharing (or breaking, if you will,) bread (a baguette) with a crush of mine, not much
unlike how I imagine the lady and the tramp first brushed lips slurping on a fine strand of lightly
sauced spaghett. I wonder if people in France bring out baguettes with burning candles instead of birthday
cakes. Some breads are cakes in essence, but it would be pretty weird to put cheese on banana bread,
for example. In my opinion, it would be weird to put cheese on apple pie, but I guess people still do that.
To conclude, 13 year olds are annoying, wine is a truth serum, and it honestly goes great with bread.
Here are some things that I love more than bread though:

  1. The Internet
  2. kissing Real people
  3. reading books that make me feel dumb
  4. my mom, but last on this list

Anyways, have you ever thought about how beer is effectively just bread water...?
